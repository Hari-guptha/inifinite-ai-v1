UNIT -I [REVIEW OF NUMBER SYSTEMS]

This unit deals with the types of number systems like decimal octal and hexadeciamal. It also deals with 1s and 2s compliment.

UNIT- II [BOOLEAN FUNCTON AND COMBINATIONAL LOGIC DESIGN]

This unit deals with k-map QuineMcCluskey method. Then combinational circuits along with adders and subtractors.

UNIT -III [SEQUENTIAL LOGIC DESIGN]

This unit deals with types of latches and types of flip-flops and counters like asynchronous and synchronous.

UNIT -IV [RECONFIGURATION DIGITAL CIRCUITS]

This unit deals with the study of RAM, ROM and PAL, PLA.

UNIT -V [VHDL]

This unit gives an introduction to hardware description language and VHDL along with signals and data types.
