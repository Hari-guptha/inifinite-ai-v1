UNIT -I [DC CIRCUITS]

This unit deals with the study of direct current and some of the law's like kirchoff. It also discuss about the techniques involved in analysing the dc circuits.


UNIT- II [AC CIRCUITS]

This unit deals with the study of alternating currents and their behaviours. This unit also describes about the type of circuits like RL, RC, R-L-C.


UNIT- III [ELECTRICAL SAFETY AND ELECTRICAL MACHINES]

This unit is divided into 2 parts, the first deals with electrical safety like types of wiring and sensors. The second part deals with electrical machines like generator , alternator.

UNIT -IV [SEMICONDUCTORS]

This unit deals with the semiconductor types like p-type n-type, and also the diode types and their applications.

UNIT-V [TRANSISTORS]

This unit deals with the transistor types like BJT, JFET, MOSFET types transistors

UNIT-VI [Communication systems]

This unit deals with the types of communication like wired and wireless, satellite and microwave. It also deals with optical fibre communication.
